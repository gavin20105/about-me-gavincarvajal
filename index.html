My Favorite Athletes

Stephen Curry — 3-Pointers Made Per Season

The navy line shows how many three-pointers Steph Curry made each season, and the gold bars give those numbers context by showing how many games he played. The two move together at the extremes — his 2019-20 season craters on both because he played only 5 games — so a low total often reflects missed games, not lost form. (They don't track perfectly, though: in 2015-16 and 2016-17 he played the same 79 games but made 402 versus 324.) That 402 in 2015-16 was an NBA record and stands out as a peak he hasn't matched since. But across his healthy seasons he's stayed at a high, consistent level — including 357 makes in 2023-24 — so the chart shows durable, elite production rather than a steady decline.
